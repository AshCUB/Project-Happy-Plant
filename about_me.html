Hi I'm Ash!
I am a passionate, dedicated individual currently in my senior year at the University of Colorado Boulder, wrapping up a Creative Technology and Design degree through the school of Engineering. I also work as a graphic designer for the CU Boulder Environmental Center. I assist a variety of clients in creating visually engaging graphics promoting various events, services, and resources the Environmental Center has to offer. I am dedicated to staying true to my clients' visions and creating graphics that align with their individual needs. I have created projects such as posters, banners, booklets, pamphlets, handouts, utensil cases, and display cases. I feel that effective design is the key to making the ordinary extraordinary, and I am proud to contribute to an area I feel so incredibly passionate about!
