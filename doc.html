From Humble Beginnings
Project happy plant has been in development for many months now. Through the collaboration with local plant shop Moss Houseplants we have been able to create something truly unique. Here you can explore early prototypes of what is now the Happy Planter and Happy Tracker. Many aspects of this product have changed since its initial prototype, from materials and circuitry to the code and overall design. All of these tests and prototypes have allowed us to make Project Happy Plant into what it is today.
