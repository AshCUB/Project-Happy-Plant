What is Project Happy Plant?
Project Happy Plant is a mission to provide species-specific, hassle-free plant care while fostering one's connection to nature. There are two parts to this project: the Happy Planter, a uniquely designed planter with a built-in watering system that provides bottom-up watering for optimal root development; and the Happy Tracker, a kit including stickers, log sheets, and a poster to help you track your plant’s growth. Through the collaboration with a local plant shop, Project Happy Plant has made keeping our plants healthy and happy easier than ever!
